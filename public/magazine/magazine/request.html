Fisher House Foundation Privacy Policy

Fisher House Foundation is committed to protecting your privacy and does not use this web site to collect any personally identifiable information about you other than what you provide voluntarily. We do not sell, trade, or provide your name or contact information with any other organization or individual, except in those cases when a donor designates his or her contribution to a specific Fisher House.

In those cases, we may provide contact information to the manager of the designated Fisher House in order to acknowledge the donation. Managers are told they may not use this information for any type of solicitation.

We do not use your personal information for direct mail, telephone solicitation, or any other purpose other than to mail each donor a complimentary copy of Fisher House Magazine. Should you not want to receive a copy of our magazine, we will delete your name and address upon receiving a request for deletion by using the above form.

We do not use cookies. For organizations linked to our web site, please refer to their privacy statements as contained on their websites. For individuals using our web site for donating, we use a service called "Give Direct," operated and maintained by Local and Independent Charities of America. Every secure page on the lic.org website has been secured with a digital certificate by VeriSign, Inc. The privacy policy for Give Direct can be found here.

If you have questions regarding our Privacy Statement or its implementation, please contact us at info@fisherhouse.org, or by mail at 111 Rockville Pike, Suite 420, Rockville, Maryland 20850.
